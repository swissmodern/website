collection swissmodern

Swissmodern offers you 20th Century objects and design, with an emphasis on design by Swiss architects or produced in Switzerland.

authenticity

Museum quality is a term that is very important for us. Therefore, we try to restore as little as possible — the patina is a part of the history of an object and the story that it tells. Pieces that can not be left in their original condition will undergo a very sensitive professional restoration.

your shelter

We hope that you might be able to create your own environment, your home. A place that not only stimulates you visually but also inspires you and gives you a shelter in a much wider sense. We can provide you our own glimpse of inspired living, but we hope to encourage you and support you with our collection.

tribute to our heroes

We pay homage to our heroes we admire. Architects whose creations challenge us, make us dream and whose resilient ideas still have an influence on generations.
